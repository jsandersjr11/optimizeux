Common Questions

Why wouldn't I just hire a full-time designer?

Excellent point! Firstly, employing a full-time senior-level designer now demands a budget surpassing $100,000 annually, and that's before factoring in benefits (not to mention the challenge of finding one available). Furthermore, there may be periods when you can't consistently keep them occupied, leading to you incurring costs for unutilized time.

With the monthly plan, you have the flexibility to pause and restart your subscription as required, ensuring you only pay your designer when there's actual work for them

Is there a cap on the number of requests I can make?

Upon subscribing, you have the freedom to add an unlimited number of design requests to your queue, and they will be fulfilled one by one.

What is the turnaround time for receiving my designs?

Typically, most requests are fulfilled in just two days or less, but it's worth noting that more intricate tasks may require additional time

Who makes up the design team?

It might come as a surprise, but OptimizeUX is actually a one-person agency. This means you'll be working directly with me, the founder of OptimizeUX. However, for advanced requests like animations or custom illustrations, we collaborate with partner designers.

How does the pause feature work?

We recognize that you might not have a constant stream of design work throughout the entire month. You might only have one or two requests at the moment. This is where the pause feature proves beneficial.

Billing cycles are structured around a 31-day period. For instance, if you register and utilize the service for 21 days, and then choose to pause your subscription, it means the billing cycle will be put on hold. You'll then have 10 days of service remaining, which you can use at any time in the future.

What programs do you design in?

The majority of requests are created using Figma.

How can I make design requests?

OptimizeUX provides a wide range of options for requesting designs through Trello. Clients commonly make requests directly via Trello, share Google docs or wireframes, or even create a brief Loom video (for those who prefer not to write out their briefs). Essentially, if it can be linked or shared in Trello, it's fair game.

What if I don't like the design?

Not a problem! We'll keep refining the design until you're completely happy with it.

Are there any types of design work that you don't handle?

Certainly. OptimizeUX does not include the following types of design work: 3D modeling, animated graphics (GIFS, etc.), document design (such as medical forms, ect.), complex packaging, and extensive print design (like magazines, books, ect.).

What if I only have one request?

No problem at all. Once your current request is completed, you can pause your subscription and return whenever you have more design needs. You don't have to let the remaining subscription time go to waste.

Is there a refund policy in case I'm dissatisfied with the service?

As the work is high quality, we do not offer refunds.
