Common Questions

What is CRO? (Conversion Rate Optimization)

CRO, or Conversion Rate Optimization, is the process of optimizing your website to increase the percentage of visitors who take a desired action, such as making a purchase, signing up for a newsletter, or downloading a file. By understanding user behavior and running A/B tests, CRO helps businesses improve website performance and drive more revenue.

What are the different CRO service tiers you offer?

We offer three CRO service tiers to cater to different business needs:

  • Ignite (2-3 Tests/Month): Ideal for businesses new to CRO or with limited testing needs. This plan helps you get started with A/B testing and gain initial conversion insights.pen_spark

  • Ascend (Up to 6 Tests/Month): Our sweet spot plan! Ascend offers a personalized CRO strategy, up to 6 A/B tests per month, and actionable recommendations to unlock significant conversion growth.

  • Scale (6+ Tests/Month): Designed for high-volume businesses requiring extensive testing. This plan provides a dedicated CRO team, unlimited A/B testing, and in-depth analytics for continuous optimization.

How do I know which CRO service tier is right for me?

The best tier for you depends on your business size, testing needs, and budget. Here's a quick guide:

  • Ignite: Ideal for smaller businesses or those just starting with CRO.

  • Ascend: Perfect for growing businesses looking for significant conversion improvement

  • Scale: Best for high-traffic websites requiring ongoing optimization and dedicated support.

What types of A/B tests do you offer?

We offer a variety of A/B testing services to optimize different website elements.

How long does it take to see results from A/B testing?

It depends on factors like website traffic and conversion goals. However, you can typically start seeing results within a few weeks to a month with regular testing. We'll continuously analyze data and adjust your tests to optimize performance.

What happens if my A/B tests don't show significant results?

Don't worry! Even negative results provide valuable insights. We'll work with you to analyze the data, identify areas for improvement, and refine your testing strategy. Continuous iteration is key to successful CRO.

Who will be managing my A/B tests?

This depends on your chosen plan. With Ignite, you manage your tests with our support. Ascend provides dedicated CRO specialist guidance, while Scale offers a full CRO team for ongoing optimization.
